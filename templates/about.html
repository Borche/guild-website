About Deorum

Deorum on battle.net: http://eu.battle.net/wow/en/character/the-venture-co/Umas/simple

We're a relatively new guild that aims to become a closeknit group that know eachother well and enjoy playing together in Warlords of Draenor. We're not a hardcore raiding guild and we probably never will be. Our concern is to have a balance between progressing in raids, and just having fun and enjoying the social aspects of World of Warcraft. Playing and progressing together as a group is what we enjoy the most.

We will be raiding two evenings per week in Warlords of Draenor, probably on wednesdays and sundays. More info will be determined later.

To join us you have to be at least 18 years old.

For further information, contact us in game:
Guild Master: Umas
Officers: Vathas, Artulis, Ceus
